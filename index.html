What is a music??

Music is a way to express anything. We can find music in nature around us from chirping of birds to a mother's lullaby.

Types of people in vocal music field and types of music??

1. Amateurs: The people who have no experience or knowledge of music.They are beginners. 2. Intermediate : The people who have little knowledge in music. 3. Expert : The people who are expert in music field and have good amount of knowledge in music.

There are many types of music some of them are; 1.Classical: This consist of two types: i) Hindustani ii) Carnatic 2. Folk: These consist of local folklore, usually sung by our grandmothers, fathers, mothers etc. 3. Movie songs: As the name suggests these are music which is from movies.
